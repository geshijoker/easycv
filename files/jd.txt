The Team

Celonis AI Group (CeloAI) is dedicated to developing state-of-the-art AI/ML (and broadly intelligent) technologies for Celonis products (e.g., the Execution Management System / EMS) and empower enterprises and institutions to make intelligent business decisions and achieve operational excellence. We are a global organization with presence in both Germany and the US. CeloAI US has its main presence in New York, NY but also remote presences across the US.

The Role

We are seeking motivated and talented machine learning interns to join our team. The internship offers an opportunity to work on state-of-the-art machine learning techniques, including but not limited to generative AI techniques, as well as real-world applications. The intern will work with experienced professionals to contribute to the innovative machine learning related techniques and products.

As a machine learning intern, you are expected to conduct research and experiments on challenging machine learning problems that are closely related to Celonis products. You are expected to develop machine learning algorithms, models and systems for real-world applications. You may need to interact closely with other engineers to build scalable and high quality AI/ML features and solutions for our customers.

The work may span the areas of generative AI, natural language processing, data mining, recommendation, knowledge base, business intelligence, and other AI technologies.

About Us

Its Process Intelligence platform uses industry-leading process mining technology and AI to present companies with a living digital twin of their end-to-end processes.
